When and why did you stop eating meat?

I started transitioning to vegetarianism back in 2019, having an autoinmune disease weakining my body I decided to take action and radically change my diet.

I've always cared for the planet, and knew stopping eating meat would lower my carbon footprint, so I had another good reason to quit meat. After watching some documentaries about factory farming it became clear that I could no longer ignore how damaging the meat and dairy industry were, so I started my food blog called @maisonverde and spreading tips and recipes about how to succesfully transition into a whole foods plant based diet.

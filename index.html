About me

Hello there! My name is Allison and I'm a passionate home cook that enjoys experimenting with new ingredients. When cooking I like to make sure I'm nourishing my family with whole plant-based foods, My interest in cooking started as I decided to become a vegetarian for health and environmental reasons. Suddenly a new world of opportunities and flavors was revealed to me. I quickly realized vegetables are so delicious and versatile that we don't really need meat to be well-nourished.

sustainability

I believe in doing as little harm as possible in our life time and this extends to myself, other people, animals, and the planet. Vegetarianism begins with compassion towards other living beings and expands into all aspects of my lifestyle. Along the way, I've learned to reduce my carbon footprint by generating as little waste as possible and taking everyday actions that create a positive impact.
